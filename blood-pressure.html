How to Measure

1

Prepare

Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measuring. Avoid caffeine, exercise, and smoking for 30 minutes before.

2

Position

Sit with your back supported, feet flat on the floor, and arm supported at heart level.

3

Measure

Place the cuff on your bare upper arm. Take two readings 1 minute apart and record the average.

Tips for Accurate Readings

  • Use the same arm each time
  • Measure at the same time each day
  • Empty your bladder before measuring
  • Don't talk during the measurement
